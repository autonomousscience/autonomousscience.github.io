The Collaborative Steering Council serves as AISLE's primary governance body, composed of diverse representatives from participating organizations who share equal decision-making authority. The Council's mission is to guide AISLE's strategic direction, foster cross-institutional collaboration, establish shared technical standards, and ensure the network remains aligned with its vision of accelerating scientific discovery through interconnected autonomous laboratories. Council members collectively identify opportunities for innovation, allocate resources to priority initiatives, and champion the expansion of the AISLE ecosystem while preserving the grassroots, distributed nature of the network.
